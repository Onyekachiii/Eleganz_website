ABOUT ELEGANZ FABRICS

Eleganz Fabrics is a continously growing brand which launched its first appearance in the textile market in 2016. Since then it has expanded and currently operates in Western Africa, Southern Africa and the Indian ocean with operational bases in Nigeria, South Africa and Mauritius Island. it has also succeeded in creating and co-creating an ever evolving fashion sense through meeting-up with customers' expectations by delivering top quality and exquisite textile prints with the finest, purest 100% cotton fabrics.

ELEGANZ FABRICS is a subsidiary of ELEGANZ RECREATION LTD

At Eleganz Fabrics, we value our community, support their fashion needs and reward their fashion tastes through high quality and contemporary designs. Our services reflect our values and what we stand for via customer commitment. We develop relationships beyond sales from start which is purchase of the fabric to finish which is the desire of getting a perfect outfit.

INTEGRITY

We uphold high standard of integrity by offering full money back and exchange guarantees in cases of unforseen manufacturing fabric errors and other unintended explanable errors

CULTURE

Not the least by any means; we strive everyday to promote and show our Motherland 'Africa' culture to the globe through our home initiated designs from sub-saharan and other parts of Africa to the world by offering rich colourful and exquisite African textile prints. above all, we tell our stories through our trendy and colourful prints.

OUR OBJECTIVE

We provide exquisite and contemporary Motherland African colourful textiles which not only tell stories but feed our customers' tastes with quality and style.
